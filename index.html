About Goldbourne Finance

Licensed microfinance institution providing financial solutions across Zimbabwe

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To participate in the overall economic and social development of the nation by mobilizing savings and human resources and to encourage thrift among clients thereby creating a source of credits for all clients.

Mission: To be the best microfinance company in Zimbabwe and to serve everyone in need.

Our History

Goldbourne Finance is a registered microfinance company in Zimbabwe, licensed by the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe. Established on December 10, 2019, we provide various loan services including cash loans, educational loans, gadget loans, and business loans.

Initially starting with a team of 5 directors and 3 employees, we've experienced significant growth and now command a 25% market share in Masvingo. With multiple branches across the region, we continue to expand our reach to serve more clients.
